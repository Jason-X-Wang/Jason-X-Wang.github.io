I am delighted to announce that the **University of Sheffield Management School** will be hosting the upcoming installment of the **N8 Operations, Supply Chain and Logistics Management (OSCM) Research Seminar Series** on **Tuesday, 21st April 2026**.

The N8 OSCM Seminar Series is a prestigious collaborative initiative hosted on a rolling basis by the eight universities of the [N8 Research Partnership](https://www.n8research.org.uk/). Our subject groups, **OMDS** (Operations Management and Decision Sciences) and **SOLARC** (Sheffield Ocean, Logistics and Supply Chain Research Centre), are proud to represent Sheffield in this regional rotation.

### Featured Keynote: Prof. Dmitry Ivanov
For our Sheffield session, we are honored to welcome **[Professor Dmitry Ivanov](https://www.hwr-berlin.de/hwr-berlin/ueber-uns/person/detail/286-dmitry-ivanov)** from the Berlin School of Economics and Law. 

Prof. Ivanov is a world-leading authority on supply chain resilience and digital twins. His pioneering work on the "ripple effect" and viable supply chains has shaped modern thinking on global logistics. During the seminar, he will present his latest research on navigating complexity and disruption in the digital age.

### Event Details
* **Date:** Tuesday, 21st April 2026
* **Format:** Hybrid (In-person at Sheffield and Online via Zoom)
* **Host Chairs:** Prof. Francisco Saldanha-da-Gama (Chair) and Dr. Jason X. Wang (Deputy Chair)

This seminar represents a fantastic opportunity for researchers and PhD students across the N8 network to engage with cutting-edge academic insights and foster new collaborations. 

Further details regarding the specific room location and registration links will be shared shortly. I look forward to welcoming colleagues from across the North of England to Sheffield this April!
