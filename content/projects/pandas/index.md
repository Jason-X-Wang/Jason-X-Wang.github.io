**Current Status:** Under second-round review at *Journal of Business Research* (ABS: 3, ABDC: A, JIF: 10.5).

### Project Overview
This research explores the transition from interpersonal/inter-organizational trust to "system-based trust" enabled by blockchain ledgers. By conducting a multi-case study within the pharmaceutical sector, we identify how distributed ledgers mitigate counterfeit risks and improve regulatory compliance.
