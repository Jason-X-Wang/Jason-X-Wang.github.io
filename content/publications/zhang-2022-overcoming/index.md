abstract: "Excess greenhouse gas emissions from human and industrial activities are believed to be the key cause of catastrophic climate change. This pioneering study addresses the knowledge gap by case studies of six first movers. We find four common barriers: major upfront investment costs, lack of awareness, lack of expertise, and resistant mindset. We find that most first movers achieved positive economic performance associated with their carbon neutrality initiatives."
